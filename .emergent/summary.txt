<product_requirements>
The Koala Mining application is a Bitcoin mining simulator with real financial integrations, rebranded from Bitcoin Mining. Users can simulate BTC mining, view hash rates, and manage miners via a 4-tab navigation (dashboard, store, invites, profile). Key features include Google/email authentication, a black marble and gold theme, a Forgot Password flow, and real BTC withdrawals (min 0.00001 BTC, 0.5% fee to a specific address). Purchased miners auto-activate, and real-time balance updates (1-second) and 14-decimal BTC display are crucial. Monetization is through real Bitcoin withdrawals (via Kraken API) and PayPal for miner purchases, with Google AdMob (previously Facebook Ads) integrated for app launch, ad miners, and withdrawal clicks. Ad limits are 0-30 daily, with non-rewarded ads for launch/withdrawal.
